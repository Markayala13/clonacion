ABOUT MAPLES MOTORSPORTS

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by veteran racer and entrepreneur Michael Maples, Maples Motorsports represents the culmination of decades of racing passion, military discipline, and business excellence. Based in Oklahoma, our team combines championship-level talent with cutting-edge technology and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

As a full-time competitor in the NASCAR ARCA Menards Series, Maples Motorsports is building a legacy of performance, integrity, and innovation. Our diverse roster of talented drivers brings together experience, youth, and determination, creating a dynamic team capable of competing at the highest levels of stock car racing.

From our state-of-the-art facilities to our experienced crew chiefs and mechanics, every aspect of Maples Motorsports is designed for one purpose: winning. We're not just racing – we're building a championship organization that will compete at NASCAR's highest levels for years to come.

MICHAEL MAPLES

Choctaw, Oklahoma

62 years old

Team Owner & Driver #99. Army veteran and oil industry entrepreneur. 7th place ARCA 2025, 8th place 2024. Founded Maples Motorsports after 30+ years racing experience. From California asphalt to ARCA championship contender.

Michael Maples is a 62-year-old racing veteran from Choctaw, Oklahoma. His passion for motorsports began in 1993 with asphalt late model racing in California. After a 21-year hiatus to focus on family, he returned to racing in 2019 with IMCA modifieds. In 2024, Michael made his dream a reality by entering the ARCA Menards Series with Fast Track Racing, finishing 8th in points. For 2025, Michael formed Maples Motorsports as both driver and owner of the #99 Chevrolet, finishing 7th in the championship. An Army veteran and oil industry entrepreneur, Michael brings discipline, experience, and championship ambition to every race.

RYAN VARGAS

La Mirada, California

25 years old

2x Wendell Scott Trailblazer Award winner and NASCAR Drive for Diversity graduate. International racing experience across Euro and Canada Series. Community champion and FACES board member inspiring young fans nationwide.

La Mirada, California's Ryan Vargas is more than just another young racer with a dream of making it to the top of NASCAR's ladder. Ryan started racing at 11 years old in 2012 and proved to be a quick learner, winning dozens of races and championships in lower divisions.

Now at 25 years old, Vargas has become a known name in the sport of NASCAR. In 2018, Ryan was signed onto the NASCAR Drive for Diversity Program and competed in the NASCAR K&N Pro Series East for Rev Racing. In 2019, Vargas made the jump to the NASCAR O'Reilly Auto Parts Series and later the Craftsman Truck Series, garnering numerous top-20's and top-10's in his young NASCAR career.

Internationally, Vargas completed stints in the NASCAR Euro and NASCAR Canada Series, placing Top-10 in the standings in both series, securing overall Podiums and a Junior Class Victory in Germany (Sep. 2024).

Ryan's impressive 10-year racing career includes the 2016 and 2017 Wendell Scott Trailblazer Award which is the highest honors given to a minority (in Ryan's case, Hispanic) or female racer who competes in the NWAAS. Ryan is the first and only driver in NASCAR history to receive the prestigious award more than once.

Along with that, Vargas continues to use his diagnosis of Craniosynostosis (a skull deformation he had surgery for at 11 months old) to inspire young fans across the country to pursue their dreams no matter what.

In November of 2023, Ryan was named the 2023 Comcast Community Champion for his efforts as a board member of FACES: The National Craniofacial Association. As part of the award, FACES was given a donation of $60,000 to go towards grants for medical travel, events and the annual FACES Camp. In January 2024, Ryan was also recognized with the 2023 NMPA Q1 Pocono Spirit Award for his contributions with FACES.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • 2x Wendell Scott Trailblazer Award Winner (2016, 2017)
  • First Hispanic Driver to Win Award Twice
  • 2023 Comcast Community Champion
  • 2023 NMPA Q1 Pocono Spirit Award
  • FACES Association Board Member
  • NASCAR Drive for Diversity Graduate

AUSTIN VAUGHN

Burnsville, Mississippi

17 years old

2025 ARCA East Rookie of the Year & 4th Place Championship finisher. From go-karts at 9 to ARCA at 15. Mississippi's youngest licensed stock car racer with pit crew experience and championship potential.

Austin's hands-on experience in the pits has given him a deeper appreciation for the sport, helping him better understand what it takes to succeed at a professional level, both on and off the track.

In 2025, Austin completed a full ARCA Menards East season along with select starts in the ARCA Menards Main Series. He finished fourth in the overall East driver standings and earned the NASCAR ARCA Menards East Rookie of the Year title.

Looking ahead to 2026, Austin's focus is on building another strong East season while gaining more experience in the Main Series as he continues his climb to the highest levels of professional racing.

SHANE BACKES

Racing since 1987

48 years old

U.S. Navy EOD veteran, Union Boilermaker, and commercial diver. 35+ years racing from IHRA drag racing to NASCAR dirt series. Boilermakers Union partnership. Blue-collar excellence meets championship determination.

Shane Backes is a seasoned ARCA driver with an incredible life story that extends far beyond the racetrack. A proud U.S. Navy veteran who served as an EOD (Explosive Ordnance Disposal) specialist, Shane brings the same precision, courage, and dedication to racing that he demonstrated in service to his country.

Currently a Union Boilermaker and commercial diver, Shane has been racing since he was 11 years old, competing in everything from IHRA Drag racing to NASCAR dirt series. His partnership with the Boilermakers Union showcases his commitment to working-class values and blue-collar excellence. Shane's decades of experience and unwavering work ethic make him a fierce competitor on any track.
